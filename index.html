What is my project?

It's a story driven 2D pixel art RPG game. For now it's called BodyFrame, but we'll see about that. The website is called BuckySide as it's currently the main village in which the game takes place.

I can't reveal much about the story, as it's the focus of the game, but I'm happy to share the mechanics that will be invovled in the game, all of the graphics and of course the features.
