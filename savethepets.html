Save the pets!

Goal of the game:

The squares represent the animals lost in the wilderness.The number tells you how many treats the animals need to return to you and the color changes the closer you get to regaining the pets trust. Save as many animals as you can!

How the game works:

At the bottom of the game screen, you can see the amount of pets you've saved as well as the amount of treats you've already fed the animals. Each new row of animals that appears will require more treats for you to feed. But don't worry, you get an extra treat each round. Also keep in mind there is level progression in the game. For example, when you save 20 pets, you advance to level 2, where each round two new roads of pets will spawn. You'll recieve an indicator in the bottom right corner, where your pet will notify you of the level up.

Instructions for the game:

Use your mouse to drag and draw a line where you want to fire your treats, release the button, when ready. The treats will bounce of the animals, once they hit the bottom, they will return and refill the treats you have to feed the animals again.
